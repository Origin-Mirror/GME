Problem

Challenge

Autoimmune destruction of pancreatic β-cells underlies T1D. Modulating specific immune checkpoints is an area of ongoing research, requiring careful design and validation of candidate molecules.

Approach

Solution (R&D)

Genesis uses AI-assisted exploration (generative models + rules-based filters + docking) to propose candidate structures and produce transparent, reproducible reports for further expert review.
